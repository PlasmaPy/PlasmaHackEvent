    Plasma Hack Week 2021: Social Events

    Since this year's Hack Week is purely virtual, and as such, we inherently lose the social aspect related to in-person events. In an effort to regain some camaraderie, we're currently scheduling social hours for every day of the event and will provide mechanisms so people can split off into their own working/social groups. Please stay tuned, and we'll update you with more info. You can also visit our scheduling page.
